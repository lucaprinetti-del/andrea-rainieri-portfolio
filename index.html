01 — About

Behind the desk

Andrea Rainieri is a music producer and sound designer working across Genova, Milan, and Venice.

He holds a Master's degree in Sound Production, Sound Design, and Music Production from Big Rock, where he developed a production approach rooted in both technical precision and song craft.

His work moves across R&B, hip-pop, and pop, shaping tracks from first idea through to a finished, release-ready mix.
